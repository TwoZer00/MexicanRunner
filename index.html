Story

After many years life has taken many blows, the economic situation does not allow you to give the maximum of your abilities and besides this you have the responsibility of a family on your back.
After several attempts in your country it is impossible for you to get over it but since you were a child you have heard about the promised land, that land that is in the north; a place where you can make your dreams come true and achieve a life of dreams and finally have your family But the laws do not allow you to arrive in the legal way to that land of happiness, with your economic capacities you will not be able to arrive in the conventional way, but that will not stop you from arriving to the land of prosperity.
There will be no obstacles that will prevent you from arriving at the place you want and obtaining the stability and happiness you have always desired, overcome the barriers that life and legal life put in your way, jump over every wall that is put in front of you, be the: MexicanRunner
